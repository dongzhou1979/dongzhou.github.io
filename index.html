Welcome to Dong Zhou's personal website!

Dr. Dong Zhou is currently a professor in School of Computer Science and Engineering (SCSE) at Hunan University of Science and Technology (HNUST). He is a PHD/Master student Supervisor, Young scholar of "Furong Scholar Award Program" of Hunan Province, Young backbone teacher of Hunan Province, High-level talents of Xiangtan City, Special professor of "Xiangjiang Scholar" of Hunan University of Science and Technology. He has published more than 90 papers in well-known international conferences and journals including IEEE TKDE, IEEE TSC, ACM Computing Surveys, ACM TALIP, ESWA, IRJ, UMUAI and CIKM, EMNLP, COLING, ICMR, ECIR, WI, etc.


His current research interests include:

  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Information Retrieval
  • Social Media and Social Computing
  • Big Data Analysis and Processing
  • Machine Learning
